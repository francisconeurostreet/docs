Firm configuration is handled in the Ignite admin dashboard.

Sandbox firms may already include starter configuration, such as a sample challenge and a standard futures symbol set. Treat that as a starting point, not as final production configuration.

## What to review

Before going live, confirm:

- Your firm slug and environment URLs.
- Challenge setup, phases, balances, and rules.
- Enabled symbols, exchanges, and products.
- Staff access and admin roles.
- User and account workflows that your integration will automate.
- Market data entitlements attached to the challenges you offer.

## Dashboard vs API

Use the dashboard for firm-level setup and operational review.

Use the API for integration workflows such as creating users, creating accounts, authorizing entitlements, and querying account or position data.

If you are unsure whether a workflow should be handled by dashboard users or by your integration, confirm it during onboarding.

<Note>
  Do not assume sandbox defaults match your production launch configuration. Review challenge, symbol, and entitlement setup before customer traffic goes live.
</Note>
